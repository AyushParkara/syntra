# Design references and attributions

This file records design and color-palette references used while creating Syntra's
terminal themes. It is an attribution record, not a legal determination of
copyright, license scope, authorship, or derivative-work status.

Syntra's source distribution does not intentionally bundle code, icons, fonts,
screenshots, or other assets from the referenced projects. Some built-in themes use
or are inspired by published color-palette values, mapped to terminal colors.

## Theme and design references

  Project: https://github.com/catppuccin/catppuccin

- **Nord** — palette reference.

- **Ghostty** — terminal aesthetic reference.

The `cosmic`, `nebula`, and `aurora` theme names refer to Syntra themes. They are
included here because their visual direction may be informed by modern terminal
design, not because they include upstream project assets.
